Brick allows you to apply filter(s) on your expressions:

<span >{{ label } | uppercase | hello}</span>

just by doing: 

view.filter('hello', function(str) {
 return 'hello ' + str + '!';
});

Filters and expressions are a convenient way to format your data. However, we advise you to keep your templates as clean as possible and to filter/compute/format your data programmaticaly : brick inherits from Store which allows you to do all of that in JavaScript.
